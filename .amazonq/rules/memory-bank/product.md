# Trendy Threads - E-commerce Management System

## Project Purpose
Trendy Threads is a comprehensive e-commerce platform designed for fashion retail businesses. It provides a complete solution for managing inventory, processing sales, handling customer orders, and administering business operations through both customer-facing and employee interfaces.

## Key Features

### Customer Features
- **Product Catalog**: Browse and search fashion items with detailed product information
- **Shopping Cart**: Add items to cart, modify quantities, and manage selections
- **Secure Checkout**: Process orders with integrated payment systems (PayChangu)
- **User Accounts**: Customer registration, profile management, and order history
- **Product Gallery**: Visual product displays with image galleries

### Employee/Admin Features
- **Inventory Management**: Add, edit, and delete products (bails) with image uploads
- **Sales Tracking**: Monitor sales performance with detailed analytics and charts
- **Order Processing**: Manage customer orders and payment status
- **Notification System**: Real-time notifications for business activities
- **Dashboard Analytics**: Comprehensive business insights and reporting
- **User Management**: Handle customer accounts and employee access

### Technical Capabilities
- **Multi-Image Support**: Upload and manage multiple product images with primary image selection
- **Payment Integration**: PayChangu payment gateway integration with webhook support
- **Responsive Design**: Bootstrap-based UI with Material Design components
- **Database Management**: MySQL database with automated table creation and migration scripts
- **File Upload System**: Secure image upload with validation and organization
- **Session Management**: Secure user authentication and session handling

## Target Users
- **Fashion Retailers**: Small to medium-sized clothing businesses
- **Inventory Managers**: Staff responsible for product catalog management
- **Sales Teams**: Employees tracking sales performance and customer orders
- **Business Owners**: Administrators needing comprehensive business oversight
- **Customers**: End-users shopping for fashion items online

## Use Cases
- Managing fashion inventory with detailed product information and images
- Processing online orders with secure payment handling
- Tracking sales performance and generating business reports
- Handling customer inquiries and order management
- Maintaining product catalogs with seasonal updates
- Monitoring business metrics and performance analytics
